OUR STORY

Students today face packed schedules that push personal health aside leading to irregular sleep, minimal exercise, and poor hydration. We created AETHERNA to make healthy habits simple, trackable, and actually sustainable.

A world where every student has the support to live healthier, more balanced lives.

Empower students with tools, knowledge & motivation to make wellness a natural part of daily life.
