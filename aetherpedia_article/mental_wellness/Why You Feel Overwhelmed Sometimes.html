Why You Feel Overwhelmed Sometimes

By Yuan Ge • Mental Health

Feeling overwhelmed is common, especially for teenagers and young adults. School pressure, social expectations, and daily responsibilities can build mental fatigue over time.

When everything piles up, your brain becomes overloaded. This can lead to stress, anxiety, and difficulty focusing on tasks.

Taking small breaks, organizing your tasks, and maintaining healthy habits like sleep and exercise can help reduce mental overload.
